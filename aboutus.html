Our Story

Ryan the founder of Private Duty Billing Solutions, draw from their experience as former owners and employees of a home care agency to understand the unique challenges of small business ownership. Recognizing the need for specialized support in billing and operations, they launched Private Duty Billing Solutions in Kalamazoo, MI. With a commitment to empowering home care agencies, they provide tailored consultation and support to help them thrive in an evolving industry.

Driven by their firsthand knowledge and passion for making a difference, Ryan, lead their team in delivering unparalleled assistance to home care agencies. Whether it's optimizing billing processes or streamlining operations, Private Duty Billing Solutions is dedicated to providing the tools and expertise needed for success in the dynamic world of home care.
