Services

Below are the services we offer at a baseline. We are a firm believer in custom solutions to cases that we believe are applicable. It is of upmost important that we are customizing solutions for all clients to meet the individual needs that we know all businesses and business owners have.

Cash Flow Management

In our experience many clients do not realize they have payors that can and will pay more quickly. Running a Home Care agency has tight margins and your growth is dependent upon your ability to quickly and efficiently turn receivables into cash.

Instant Pay

Instant pay is a solution to have a portion of your recievables advanced to you at the beginning of each billing period by Private Duty Billing Solutions. This service comes at a fee and requires billing history. This is something that can be essential for groiwing home care agencies who are struggling with Cash Flow Management.

Payor Integration

In our pursuit to streamline payor integrations, our foremost objective is efficiently enrolling them into billing systems designed for each payor type.

We have had success with:
• VA
• Medicaid
• Long Term Care Insurance
• Auto Insurance
• Private Pay
• Workmans Comp
• DD programs and More!

Financial Reporting

Without a clear view into your cash flow you are basically sailing a ship without a compass. Our reporting options will allow you to have improved insight into your agency's financial situation.
